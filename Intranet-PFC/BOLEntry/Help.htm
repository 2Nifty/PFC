BOL Charge Entry Help

This function is used to enter charges associated with Bills of Lading (BOL)

Enter a BOL number In the BOL box
Enter BOL Date
Enter Charge Amount to spread by weight
Enter Charge Amount to spread by amount
Click the button that says OK

Response BOL Added indicates you have successfully added the BOL Information
Response BOL Already Created indicates you are trying to Add a BOL already in the system

To Delete a BOL and Add again, Enter JUST the BOL Number, then click Delete

Click the List Button to show All Bills of Lading in BOL No. within descending Date Order
NOTE: The Total BOL Weight and Amount are populated by the first run of the Avg Cost system after the BOL is entered. It is normal to see zeroes in these columns
