 Customer Contact

  • Customer Contact allow users to modify, create and delete Customer related contacts quickly and efficiently without having to access the Customer Master application
  • Customer Contact application can be found under two menus: Branch Operations and Marketing Management inside of Business Management which will be available based on the access rights, authorization and privileges of the User

    Step 1: Click on the Business Management Menu Tab

    Step 2: Click on the Menu Category Branch Operations or Marketing Management that is displayed in side menu. Customer Contact is available in both these menu categories to the User according to their access rights

    Step 3: Select Customer Contact from the options displayed after selecting Branch Operations from the menu.

Step 4: Now you will be directed to the Customer Contact screen. It has three sections to display the Customer information, Contacts and section to view or edit Customer contact information

Step 5: User can search any customer record by typing the Customer Number in Customer Number textbox

Step 6: If the entered Customer Number is available in the records, Customer information and Contacts information will be displayed

Step 7: For an existing Customer, Contact details can be added or edited. Click on Add button in the Contacts section. The Customer Contact entry area becomes available for a new record. The save button becomes available. Enter the details in the Enter Customer Contact Information section

Step 8: Click on Save button to update the information. You can also alter or remove the Contacts for the Customer by clicking on Edit or Delete links of the existing contacts

Step 9: Clicking on Edit of a contact moves record to data entry area for modifications. Save after editing the details.

Step 10: Click Close button to close the Customer Contact screen.
